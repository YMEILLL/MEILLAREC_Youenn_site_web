My hobbies and passions

Table Tennis

My ultimate passion lies in the swift and dynamic world of table tennis competitions, where I find joy in the adrenaline of quick exchanges on the table while constantly refining my technique for success.

SimRacing

In simracing, my focus is on maintaining concentration, keenly listening to the virtual co-pilot's instructions in rally simulations, creating a realistic and immersive experience that mirrors the challenges of real-life rally racing.

Motorsports

In the thrilling world of motorsport, the impressive art of vehicle control during drift competitions has become my source of inspiration, with idols like Jason Banet showcasing unparalleled skill. Meanwhile, in the rally scene, the legendary Ari Vatanen's daring jumps and masterful handling continue to captivate my admiration.
